About Me

We are a team of four driven students who first joined forces in the high-pressure, high-energy environment of our school’s annual hackathon. What began as a shared interest in technology quickly evolved into a focused mission to solve real-world problems through code and creative design. Coming from different academic backgrounds, we realized early on that our combined strengths—spanning logic, aesthetics, and strategy—made us a formidable unit capable of turning abstract ideas into functional prototypes. Our project is the result of countless hours of brainstorming, late-night debugging sessions, and a relentless commitment to innovation. During the competition, we challenged ourselves to push beyond the basics of standard software, integrating sleek user interfaces with efficient back-end architecture. This journey hasn't just been about building an app; it’s been an intensive masterclass in rapid prototyping and agile development, forcing us to pivot when things broke and celebrate every successful line of code. Beyond the technical hurdles, this experience has been defined by our growth as a team. We learned that the best solutions come from diverse perspectives and that clear communication is just as vital as clean syntax. Whether we are whiteboarding a new feature or troubleshooting a persistent bug, our goal remains the same: to create impactful technology that makes a difference. We are excited to present our work and show what four students can achieve when they combine their passion with a bit of competitive spirit.
